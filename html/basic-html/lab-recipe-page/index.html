Egg Bread

This is a recipe I've seen Pinky Tai make for the first time I visited their home in Bhopar Gaon. This is a quick recipe with minimal ingredients, and if you want you can extend the recipe with things you like such as different toppings, different type of bread etc.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Oil
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs as per requirements in bowl and add spices asper liking
  2. Dip the bread in the made mixture of eggs and soak the bread properly.
  3. In a pan add some oil, and once at right temperature Put the Bread and properly cook on both sides one by one.
  4. Your delicious Egg Bread is ready
